## Common Tasks - Quick Guide

### How to Add a CVR
1. Open a deal by clicking on its ticker
2. Click the "CVRs" tab
3. Click "Add CVR" button (top right)
4. Fill in:
   - CVR Name (e.g., "FDA approval milestone payment")
   - Payment Amount (in dollars)
   - Probability (0-100%)
   - Payment Deadline (optional)
   - Notes (optional)
5. Click "Add CVR"

### How to Edit a CVR
1. Open a deal → Click "CVRs" tab
2. Click "Edit" button next to the CVR you want to change
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Save Changes"

You can also delete CVRs from the edit page (there's a confirmation dialog).

### How to Update Prices
1. Open a deal
2. Click "Update Price" button (top right)
3. Enter the new target price (and acquiror price if applicable)
4. Select the date
5. Click "Add Price"

### How to Edit Deal Terms
1. Open a deal
2. Click "Edit Deal" button (top right)
3. Update any fields (cash per share, stock ratio, dates, risk assessments, etc.)
4. Click "Save Changes"
